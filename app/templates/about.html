About Us

Welcome to Mary Angels

Welcome to Mary Angels Montessori School—where little minds grow with big dreams! Founded in 2023, we are dedicated to nurturing young learners through a joyful, safe, and stimulating environment. At Mary Angels, we embrace each child's uniqueness, encouraging curiosity, confidence, and a lifelong love for learning through play-based and personalized education. Our qualified educators support every child's academic, social, and emotional development with structured activities and meaningful connections. Whether your child is beginning their journey or preparing for the next step, we're here to make these early years memorable, fun, and foundational. Let’s grow, learn, and discover—together!
