Age-Specific Programs

Tailored learning experiences for every developmental stage

Infant Care

6 months - 2 years

Our infant program provides a nurturing environment where babies can explore and develop at their own pace. Our trained caregivers focus on sensory stimulation, motor skills development, and forming secure attachments.

  • Personalized care routines
  • Sensory development activities
  • Early language development
  • Motor skills enhancement

Toddler Care

2 years - 3 years

Our toddler program fosters independence and curiosity. Children engage in structured play-based learning that enhances their cognitive, social, and physical development.

  • Self-help skills development
  • Language enrichment activities
  • Social skill building
  • Potty training support
  • Introduction to basic concepts

Pre-KG

3 years - 4 years

Our Pre-KG program introduces children to a structured learning environment while maintaining a play-based approach. Children develop pre-literacy and pre-numeracy skills while enhancing their creativity and social abilities.

  • Phonics introduction
  • Number recognition
  • Fine motor skill activities
  • Creative expression
  • Cooperative play

Junior & Senior KG

4 years - 6 years

Our kindergarten program prepares children for primary school through a balanced curriculum that builds academic readiness, independence, and social-emotional skills.

  • Early reading and writing
  • Mathematical concepts
  • Science exploration
  • Problem-solving skills
  • School readiness activities
